## 研究主线：让AI系统服务真实的人

<div class="research-map">
<div class="map-item">
  <div class="map-title">社会智能与可靠AI</div>
  <div class="map-text">面向心理健康社区、算法公平、交通预测等场景，构建可解释、可评估、可部署的方法。</div>
  <span class="tag gold">Social Good</span><span class="tag gold">Fairness</span>
</div>
<div class="map-item">
  <div class="map-title">AI辅助编程教育</div>
  <div class="map-text">研究学生如何借助AI学习编程，何时形成脚手架，何时变成认知卸载与验证负担。</div>
  <span class="tag">AI Education</span><span class="tag">Programming</span>
</div>
<div class="map-item">
  <div class="map-title">AI介导沟通与隐私</div>
  <div class="map-text">研究AI写作、情感表达、用户同意边界与隐私选择中的信任机制。</div>
  <span class="tag blue">HCI</span><span class="tag blue">Privacy</span>
</div>
</div>
