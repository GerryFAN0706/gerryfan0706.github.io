This paper investigates how AI-assisted romantic communication affects perceived authorship and authenticity. Through two studies (N=152 instrumented authoring + N=704 mixed-effects experiment), we demonstrate a competence-integrity dissociation in AI-assisted romantic messages.

**Keywords:** Computer Mediated Communication, Emotion/Affective Computing, Intimate Relationships, AI-Assisted Writing, Authorship Attribution

**Subcommittee:** Interaction Beyond the Individual; Understanding People: Statistical & Quantitative Methods
