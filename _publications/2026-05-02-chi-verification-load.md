A mixed-methods study comparing Inline, Chat, and Structured AI coding assistant modes. This paper introduces the "verification-load" index capturing failures, churn, and context switches to understand when AI assistance becomes counterproductive.

**Keywords:** Programming/Development Support, Workplaces, AI Assistance, Verification Load, Developer Experience
